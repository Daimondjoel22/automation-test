"1957 Victim Names Dictionary" since 2000 began to collect, edit, due to the continuous evolution of editing ideas, tailoring methods, but also due to the lack of time and resources, so more than ten years of style, format, arrangement, inevitably there are inconsistent situations, errors, omissions, and even irregularities, these readers are more critical and corrected, and gradually corrected and overcome.

The project has received the care and guidance of experts and scholars such as Professor Ding Shu; the help of teachers of the China Research Service Center of the University of Chinese of Hong Kong and colleagues of the Wuqi Society; and the support of the victims and their descendants in providing historical materials and memoirs in 1957.
