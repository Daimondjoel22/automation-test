OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy discloses the privacy statement for https://stip-demo.web.app/home, the Chinese Rightists Archive website. We respect your privacy rights and recognize the importance of protecting personal information. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, share, and secure that information. By visiting and using this website, you are consenting to our collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We may also collect or receive information from third parties, such as Facebook or other third-party social media sites

What personal information do we collect?

We collect certain information regarding your use of https://stip-demo.web.app/home, such as your IP address and browser. Your session and the pages you visit on https://stip-demo.web.app/home will be tracked including the name of the domain from which the site was accessed, but you will remain anonymous. We may use your IP address to identify the general geographic area from which you are accessing https://stip-demo.web.app/home. We connect data from different systems but do not link IP addresses to any personal information.

We collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it to us through account creation. Such information includes your full name, username, birth date, and email address. We use the information we gather from you for systems administration purposes, abuse prevention, and to track user trends. If you send us an email, the email address you provide may be used to send you information, respond to inquiries, and/or other requests or questions. We will not share, sell, or authorize any third party to use your email address for commercial purposes without your permission. In addition, we may also use third-party vendors (such as Google Analytics) to evaluate user information to improve web services or other communications to users.

Have any questions?

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) About “Collections” Use
  1. May I share any of the “Rightist” files on my website, blog, or social media?

    Yes, as long as you comply with these Terms of Use.

  2. May I use the Chinese Rightist Archive's logo on my website?

    No, you may not use our website's logo or other trademarks without written permission from the head Admin of the Chinese Rightist Archive website. Exceptions may apply if the logo is already integrated into the documents and other content within the Chinese Rightist Archive website which is made available for sharing through embedded links.

  3. May I use the “Rightist” documents in a research paper, lesson/lecture plans, or other class work?

    Yes, we encourage you to quote, describe, and share the “Rightist” file in your research or other class project as long as you comply with these Terms of Use. Please note that some uses of our documents in class work may be considered fair use under copyright law, and it is your responsibility to determine if your use constitutes fair use. The Chinese Rightist Archive cannot assist you in this determination.

  4. Can I contribute information about a Chinese Rightist to the Chinese Rightist Archive?

    The Chinese Rightist Archive allows all account users to contribute content through our “New Rightist Contribution” upload page under Account home. You must create an account first before you upload.
